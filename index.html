The product

Three layers. One API.

Autonode sits between OEM telematics and lender credit workflows. Normalise data. Build residual models. Deliver underwriting-grade scores — as one call.

01 · Engine
Residual Value Engine

India-specific depreciation curves by make, model, variant, geography. Calibrated from marketplace data and live telematics — not borrowed from global benchmarks that don't survive Indian duty cycles.

02 · Risk
Battery Risk Scorer

SoH grading, degradation trajectory, replacement probability. The actuarial input your insurer needs to price buyback without guessing at 40–48°C summers and 250 km/day duty cycles.

03 · Integration
Lender Integration API

One call returns residual score, battery grade, LTV range, confidence interval. A1–A5 grades for the lender. Raw telemetry never leaves the OEM. DPDP-consent governed end-to-end.
